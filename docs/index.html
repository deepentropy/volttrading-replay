Modern UI, Sub-16ms Latency

Built with Rust and GPU-accelerated rendering. DOM ladder, time & sales, and charts all update in under 16ms — faster than legacy platforms.

Variable Speed Replay

Play at 0.25x to study setups in slow motion, or 10x to scan through the session. Seek to any moment with the trade activity timeline.

Time & Sales

Full tick-by-tick trade stream color-coded by side. See exactly when large prints hit the tape and at what price levels.

All the Components You Need

DOM ladder, candlestick chart, and time & sales — all synced to the replay timeline. The same windows you use in DAS or Sterling, rebuilt from scratch.

Click-to-Trade Simulation

Place limit and market orders by clicking the DOM. Track your P&L, positions, and order fills as if it were live — with zero risk.

In-App Session Downloads

Browse and download curated replay sessions of volatile small-cap tickers directly from the app. New sessions added regularly.
