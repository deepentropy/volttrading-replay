# VoltTrading Replay

Practice reading Level 2 tape like DAS Trader -- free, offline, no account needed.

VoltTrading Replay is a desktop application that replays full 14-exchange depth market data tick-by-tick. Built for active day traders and scalpers who want to practice tape reading after hours.

## Features

- **Full Depth DOM** -- 14-exchange Level 2 book, click any price to trade
- **Time & Sales** -- Real tick-by-tick trade flow with exchange attribution
- **Variable Speed Replay** -- Pause, slow-mo, or fast-forward through any session
- **Charts** -- Candlestick charts synced to replay time
- **Hotkeys** -- Configurable keyboard shortcuts for all trading actions
- **Simulated Execution** -- Practice entries and exits with P&L tracking

### Install Notes

The app is not code-signed yet. Your OS may show a warning on first launch:

- **Windows:** If SmartScreen appears, click "More info" then "Run anyway"
- **macOS:** If blocked, right-click the app, select "Open", then click "Open" again

## How It Works

1. Launch the app -- a bundled replay session loads automatically, paused at 9:30 AM ET
2. Press Play to watch the tape unfold in real time
3. Click prices on the DOM to practice entries and exits
4. Download additional sessions from within the app (no account required)

## Session Library

The app ships with one bundled session. More sessions are available to download directly from the app, including high-volume squeezes, large-cap momentum plays, and volatile small-caps.

New sessions are added regularly.

## Built For

Traders who use DAS Trader Pro, Sterling Pro, or Takion and want to practice tape reading outside market hours.
